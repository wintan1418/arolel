# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Arolel should be a respectful, practical place to collaborate.

Examples of behavior that contributes to a healthy project:

- Being clear and constructive
- Respecting different experience levels
- Focusing criticism on the work, not the person
- Accepting maintainer decisions about project direction

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information
- Spam or repeated off-topic comments

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject pull requests, or block contributors who do not follow this code of conduct.
